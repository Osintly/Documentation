# Redeem Your Gifts

The **Redeem** page lets you claim rewards from giveaways, campaigns, and partner promotions. This guide explains how to join promotions, what rewards you can receive, and how to redeem your code correctly.

<Info>
  Rewards are tied to specific campaign rules. Always check the giveaway post for eligibility, expiration date, and region restrictions.
</Info>

---

## How do I participate in giveaways?

To participate in giveaways, follow these steps:

1. **Watch official announcements**: Check the [Osintly Discord server](https://discord.gg/osintly), [website](https://osint.ly), and official social channels.
2. **Complete all required actions**: Some campaigns require joining a community, following a profile, or filling a short form.
3. **Verify your entry**: Make sure your username, email, or handle matches what the campaign asks for.
4. **Wait for winner confirmation**: Winners are contacted publicly (announcement) or privately (DM/email), depending on the campaign.
5. **Redeem your code before it expires**: Most codes are time-limited.

<Tip>
  Enable Discord notifications for the announcements channel so you do not miss short-duration promotions.
</Tip>

## Where can I find active promotions?

Active promotions and giveaways are usually announced in:

- **Discord**: [discord.gg/osintly](https://discord.gg/osintly)
- **Website banners**: [osint.ly](https://osint.ly)
- **Social channels**: [X](https://x.com/osintly), [TikTok](https://tiktok.com/@osintly), and [Telegram](https://t.me/osintly)

<Note>
  If you see a giveaway from an unverified account, confirm it in our official Discord before sharing details.
</Note>

## What types of gifts can I redeem?

Depending on the promotion, redeemable rewards may include:

- **Plan upgrades or trial periods**: Access to additional features for a fixed duration.
- **Credits**: Extra search credits added to your account balance. Important: spending credits requires an active subscription. You can purchase credits without an active subscription, but they cannot be spent until an active subscription is present. Promotional credits may have additional terms or expiration.
- **Community perks**: Special Discord roles or gated community channels.
- **Exclusive resources**: Tutorials, templates, or private OSINT content.

Each campaign has its own terms, so the exact reward and duration can differ.

## Where do I redeem my gift?

Use the following steps to redeem your code:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create or log in to your account">
    Sign in at [osint.ly](https://osint.ly). The reward is applied to the account currently connected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click your profile avatar in the top-right corner, then open **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Redeem">
    In **Settings**, click **Redeem** to access the code field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your code exactly">
    Paste the code exactly as received (same characters, no extra spaces), then validate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm reward application">
    A success message appears when the code is valid. Your credits, plan benefit, or perk is then applied automatically.

    For more details about credits and spending rules, see [Credits explained](/guides/credits-explained).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common redeem issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My code says invalid" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Double-check for typos, hidden spaces, or wrong characters (`O` vs `0`, `I` vs `1`). If you copied from mobile, paste into a plain text field first, then copy again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My code is expired" icon="clock">
    Some campaign codes have a hard expiration window. If the code has expired, contact support with the campaign name and your code.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I redeemed, but I do not see the reward" icon="rotate">
    Refresh your account page and check your plan/credit status. If there is still no update, contact support and include your account email plus the code used.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I won but never received a code" icon="envelope-open-text">
    Confirm the winner announcement source and check your DM/email spam folder. If needed, open a support request with your giveaway username.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
