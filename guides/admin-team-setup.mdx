## Create your team

1. Open Team settings.
2. Create a team workspace.
3. Confirm your team display name.

## Invite members

- Send invitations by email.
- Assign role and access level.
- Review pending invites regularly.

## Permission model

- Owner: full control of billing, members, and settings.
- Admin: operational team management.
- Member: investigation and project access based on permissions.

## Team best practices

- Define naming standards for projects.
- Review member access every month.
- Use project status to maintain investigation handoffs.
